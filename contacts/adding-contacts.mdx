# Adding GC and Customer Contacts to Podio

## Why Add Contacts?

The **Contacts** app stores information about General Contractors (GCs), subcontractors, suppliers, and customer contacts. Having their info in Podio means:
- Quick access to phone numbers and emails
- You can link them to projects they're involved in
- Everyone on your team can see who's who

---

## How to Add a New Contact

### Step 1: Open the Contacts App

1. In Podio, click on the **Precision Projects** workspace (top navigation)
2. Find and click the **Contacts** app

### Step 2: Create the Contact

1. Click the **"+ Add"** button (or **"Add item"**)
2. Fill in the contact's information:

**Required Fields:**
- **Name:** Full name (e.g., "Sarah Johnson")

**Recommended Fields:**
- **Job Title:** Their role (e.g., "Project Manager", "Site Superintendent")
- **Organization:** Company they work for (select from dropdown or add new)
- **Email:** Their email address
- **Phone:** Direct phone number
- **Address:** Office or site address if relevant
- **Notes:** Anything helpful (e.g., "Prefers text over email", "Handles all RFI responses")

### Step 3: Save

Click **"Save"** or **"Create"** (depending on Podio version).

Your contact is now in the system!

## Common Questions

**Q: What if the person works for multiple companies?**  
A: Pick their primary company for **Organization**. Mention the other companies in the **Notes** field.

**Q: Can I add internal PSS employees as contacts?**  
A: You *can*, but it's usually not necessary. Internal people are assigned via the **User** fields (PM, OC, FOCS, Foreman) on projects. The Contacts app is mainly for external people.

**Q: What if I don't have all their info yet?**  
A: Add what you know. You can edit the contact later to fill in missing details.

**Q: How do I find a contact later?**  
A: In the Contacts app, use the search bar at the top. Search by name, company, or even phone number.
