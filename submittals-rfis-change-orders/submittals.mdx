# Managing Submittals in Podio

## What Are Submittals?

Submittals are documents you send to the General Contractor (GC) for approval before starting work. Think of them as "here's what we plan to doâ€”can you approve it?" Examples include:
- Material specifications
- Product data sheets
- Shop drawings
- Installation procedures

Getting submittals approved is a **critical Pre-Construction task**. You can't start work until the GC signs off.

## How to Track a Submittal

### 1. Find the Submittal Task

1. Open your **Project** in Podio
2. Click on the **Pre-Construction** stage from the "Linked Stages" field
3. Look in the **Task Overview** section for tasks related to submittals
   - Example task names might include "Submittal: [Material/Product Name]" or "Submit Product Data"

### 2. Update the Task as You Work

**When you create the submittal:**
1. Open the task
2. Change **Status** to "In Progress" (yellow)
3. Upload your submittal document to the **Attachments** field
4. Add any notes in the **Notes** field (e.g., "Sent to GC via email on 3/15")

**When the GC approves:**
1. Change **Status** to "Complete" (green)
2. Upload the approved version to **Attachments**
3. Add a note: "Approved by [Name] on [Date]"

**If revisions are needed:**
1. Keep **Status** as "In Progress"
2. Add a note explaining what needs to change
3. Upload the revised version when ready

### 3. Track Dependencies

Some submittals must be approved before other tasks can start. The system handles this automatically:
- When you mark a submittal task **Complete**, any dependent tasks will automatically change from "Pending" to "In Progress"
- The person responsible for the next task gets notified

**Example:** You can't order materials until the material submittal is approved. The "Order Materials" task depends on "Submittal: Material Specs" being complete.

## Common Questions

**Q: Where do I see all submittals at once?**  
A: Open the Pre-Construction stage and filter the Task Overview by searching for "submittal" in the task names. [NEEDS VERIFICATION: Exact filter method]

**Q: What if the GC loses our submittal?**  
A: Check the task Attachments. You have a copy there. Re-send it and add a note documenting the re-submission.

**Q: Can I add my own submittal tasks?**  
A: Yes! In the Pre-Construction stage's Task Overview, click "Add Task" and create a new one. Set the **Type** to "Task" and link it to the stage.
