# Logging In to Podio

## First Time Login

1. Open your web browser (Chrome recommended)
2. Go to **podio.com**
3. Click **Log In** in the top right
4. Enter your company email (`yourname@precisionsiteservices.com`)
5. Enter your password
6. Click **Log In**

## Forgot Your Password?

1. Go to podio.com and click **Log In**
2. Click **Forgot password?**
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your inbox for a reset link
5. Click the link and create a new password

## Staying Logged In

Podio will keep you logged in on your computer. If you're on a shared computer, always log out when you're done.

**To log out:**
1. Click your profile picture (top right corner)
2. Click **Log out**

## Using Google to Log In

If your account is connected to Google:

1. Go to podio.com
2. Click **Log in with Google**
3. Select your `@precisionsiteservices.com` account
4. You're in!
